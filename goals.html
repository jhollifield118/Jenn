The Goals

What are the association's goals? What is the goal of the website?

The goal of the website is to help cancer warriors, survivors, and caregivers find CAA both on the web and by location. To provide information about CAAâ€™s services and how to get help. Also to provide accurate and new information about cancer treatments, support groups, and resources.

First, I recognized what the three main goals I believe Slow Food Upstate communicated. I did not establish these goals so that I could match them to my audience, but they served as more of loose guidlines and overarching categories.

After determining those broad goals, I made categories: Audience (as an Organization), Audience (Consumers/Earth Market), Sponsors, Partners, and Skeptics. I then proceded to fit a wide range and variety of audiences into each category.

I soon found that there were some audiences that fit into multiple categories. I wanted to cover all my bases, so I wedged them between columns to show interchangability. It was hard narrowing them down even further and deciding which section of the determined audience would fit best.

When it came down to refining my audience in this exercise, I focused on the categories Audience (as an Organization) and Audience (Consumers/Earth Market). I found that these two categories directly represented the main audience I wanted to reach.
