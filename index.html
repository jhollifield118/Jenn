Design Challenge

What are we trying to solve?

What is the promblem you're trying to solve?

To connect with and support those battling cancer and to encourage them in their journey.

1. Take a stab at framing it as a design question.

How does the Cancer Association website help people with cancer to thrive?

2. Now, state the ultimate impact you're trying to have.

Awareness of the organization and support from the community through donations.

3. What are some possible soultions to your problem?

Redesigning the website to appeal to a broader audience. Get the word out about the center's presence to volunteers and patients.

4. Finally, write down some of the context and constraints that you're facing.

It is a small association that does not have much advertising in the community. To grow our outreach and inform people of the association's location.

5. Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.

How can I create a website that appeals to everyone while reaching out to the public and benefit people battling cancer?
