Interviews

Let's ask a staff member!

I reached out through email to question a staff memeber at CAA to ask them about the website.

CAA Director: Angela Stringer

This was her response:

Interviewer: What do you think the purpose of the Cancer Association's website should be?

"The CAA website is a resource for LOCAL cancer warriors living in Anderson County. It should serve as a marketing piece for cancer warriors, caregivers and their families as well as potential and existing donors".

Interviewer: What do you think the goals of the website should be?

"To help cancer warriors, survivors, and caregivers find CAA (both on the web & location-wise). Provide information about CAA’s services and how to get help. Provide accurate & up-to-date information about cancer, treatment, etc. Provide access to support groups and individual support. Provide links to resources".

"Provide information about CAA’s services for caregivers. Provide individual & group support information/links. Provide links to resources for caregivers".

"Provide information regarding where donation funds go and what they are used for. Provide information on how donor dollars help local cancer patients, caregivers and their families. Provide an easily accessible way to give. Provide a very visible place to thank our donors."

Interviewer: Who are the primary users?

"Primary users of the CAA website are cancer warriors, cancer survivors, caregivers, families and donors those both potential and existing".
