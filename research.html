Initial Research

Initial research consits of understanding the client website and its mission.

The Cancer Association of Anderson's website is currently not designed in a way that communicates a clear message. In researching I can understand what the cancer association serves to accomplish, how they advertise, and their mission.

There were a few main issues I notice when I assess their website from a design perspective is that there is an extreme use of the color purple. It is unknown as to why they decided to choose purple, but it is constant throughout the entire site.

In resarching about the Cancer Association of Anderson, I found that they only have a Facebook page as part of their marketing and advertising strategies. However, they also direct community wide events for all to attend.
